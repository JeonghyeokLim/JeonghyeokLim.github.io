ABOUT

Research Profile

My research centers on robots that must operate reliably in complex and changing environments. I am particularly interested in combining model-based reasoning with reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and uncertainty-aware state estimation.

My work spans humanoid locomotion, industrial autonomous mobile robots, autonomous driving, and multi-agent robotic systems.

Humanoid robotics
Reusable motion representations, skill decomposition, and whole-body behavior composition
Learning-based control
Reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and robust execution under uncertainty
Autonomous systems
Planning, prediction, control, and state estimation for AMRs and autonomous vehicles
