About

Researching intelligent machines from motion to autonomy.

My work focuses on robots that must operate reliably in complex, changing environments. I am particularly interested in extracting reusable motion structure, combining model-based reasoning with learning, and developing control systems that transfer from simulation to real hardware.

My projects span humanoid locomotion, industrial mobile robots, autonomous driving, multi-agent planning, and energy-system robotics.
