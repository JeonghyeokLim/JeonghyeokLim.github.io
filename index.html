ABOUT

Research profile

My research centers on robots that must operate reliably in complex and changing environments. I am particularly interested in combining model-based reasoning with reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and state estimation.

My experience spans humanoid locomotion, industrial mobile robots, autonomous driving, multi-agent planning, and robotic energy systems.

Current focus
Reusable motor primitives for humanoid motion
Methods
Reinforcement learning, MPC, state estimation
Systems
Humanoids, AMRs, autonomous vehicles
